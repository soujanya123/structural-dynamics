Its working principle is based on the measurement of potential difference between the solution of which

  2.  Water-environment application

  3.  Petrochemical application

  4.  Food application

Mercury Intrusion Porosimeter

Quantachrome

Poremaster 33/60

 

 

Functions :-        This instrument is used for analysis of porous material by mercury     intrusion.

 

The Quantachrome Poremaster – 60 is designed to measure pore volumes in the range about 400 to 0.035 μm (35Å) diameter.

 

For measurement of volumes of pores larger than 7 μm the dual, low pressure measuring and fill station is used.

 

These stations are used to fill the sample cells with mercury and measure pore volumes in the range of about 400 to 4.3 μm diameter.
