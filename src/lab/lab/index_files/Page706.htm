Lab users should be sensitive to overall environment of the lab and the Institute and follow rules below.

1. After a test, tidy up the place.

2. Do not throw any cement waste or chemical in the sink. This may choke up or damage the drainage system.

3. For concrete waste, there is a designated place behind CSTL, please contact the lab staff to ascertain the correct place.

4. Do  not throw any used/ unused  bottles in open area since it is hazardous to the workers who lift and dispose the bottles. Students should contact the staff-in-charge of the concerned lab to dispose such waste.

5. Strict action will be taken against any student not complying with above rules.
